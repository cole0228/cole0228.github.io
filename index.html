ABOUT ME

My name is Rebecka Cole, I’ve always loved art and photography. I would go on lots of hikes as a kid and would fill the memory card on my camera by the end of the hike. I’m from the Maritimes, Fredericton, New Brunswick, this has influenced my personality a lot. Being from the Maritimes has taught me to slow down and take in everything around me.

What I love to do is branding. I enjoy creating the identity of a company, something that will be one of the main things that draws people to a company. I also enjoy bringing stories to life through my artwork and I’ve brought this idea through to any logos I create and artwork. A logo should tell a story about the company and it’s ideals in one quick look.
